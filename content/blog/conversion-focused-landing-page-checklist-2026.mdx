## Overview

Use this checklist before launching any paid campaign landing page.

## Checklist

- One clear H1 with buyer-oriented outcome
- Proof block near first CTA
- Friction-free form fields
- Mobile-first spacing and tap targets
- Page speed checks for LCP and CLS
- FAQ section for objection handling
