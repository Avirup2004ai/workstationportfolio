## Overview

This draft explains how to interpret ROI responsibly for SME marketing decisions.

## Key formulas

- Basic ROI = ((Revenue - Cost) / Cost) x 100
- Use net revenue where possible
- Segment by channel and campaign type

## Practical recommendations

- Track lead quality alongside ROI
- Separate assisted from direct conversion impact
- Use rolling 30-day and 90-day views
